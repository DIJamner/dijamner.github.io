I am a 5th year undergraduate (expected graduation May 2020) studying programming languages with Amal Ahmed at Northeastern University. My research ranges from gradual typing and parametric polymorphism to compositional compiler correctness. I am currently working on approaches to developing a mechanized framework for extension-proof compiler verification based on modular specification and reasoning about programming languages.
