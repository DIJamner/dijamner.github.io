Bio

I am an undergraduate programming languages researcher working with Amal Ahmed at Northeastern University. I do research on topics related to issues of programming language safety, expressivity, and interoperability. This ranges from gradual typing and parametric polymorphism to compositional compiler correctness. I am also currently nurturing an interest in modular programming languages in the style of Benjamin Delaware's "Modular monadic metatheory".
